Acknowledgements

Encoding the Vandegrifter was made possible thanks to several institutions, organisations, and individuals, particularly the Centre for Data, Culture, and Society, at the University of Edinburgh, which provided this project with essential funding. Technical support from EDINA, in particular Harry Newton and Andrew Horne, has ensured the functionality of this site. Many thanks also go to the Robert Louis Stevenson Museum, in St. Helena, California, for permissions to use images and content from the archives. Without the above assistance, these four manuscripts might have remained in the margins of literary history for much longer.
The site is maintained by the Co-Principal Investigators Dr. Robyn Pritzker and Dr. Anouk Lang. Dr. Pritzker has written extensively about Fanny Van de Grift Stevenson, her stories, and the rest of the Stevenson family.
